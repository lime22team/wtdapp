About

A Moment is a simple tool for moments of indecision.

It offers a single, small suggestion when you don’t know what to do next.
Nothing more.

There are no profiles, no tracking, and no notifications.
The app works entirely offline and keeps things intentionally minimal.

This is not a coaching app.
Not a therapy app.
Not a productivity system.

It’s just a quiet place to pause and move forward.
